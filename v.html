About Artemis Hospitals

Individuals with disabilities experience significant health disparities compared to the non-disabled population. Despite the documented need, the focus on health disparity issues within the disability population is limited and often ignored

There is a need for better surveillance methods or tools in public health to measure and track prevalence of disabilities and untangle congenital, acquired, and disability derived from chronic conditions
